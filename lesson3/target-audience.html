Target Audience

The target audience for this website is broad and includes people who simply want to know what the weather will be today out of curiosity, to people who need to know what the weather will be on a certain day to better plan outdoor events.


Personas

Persona 1

Persona: Mother of two children
Fictional name: Toni Rose
responsibilities: Full-time mother of two children
Demographics:
  • 28 years old
  • Married
  • Mother of two children
  • Full-time mother
Goals and tasks: Majority of her focus is on looking after the children. One of her major concerns when she leaves the house with the kids, is whether they are dressed appropriately for the weather.
Environment: She is busy with trying to juggle two children, while looking after their basic needs such as food and clothing, so she uses her phone on a regular basis because it is more convenient. She does have access to a laptop and does use it occasionally.
Quote: "What is the weather going to be today."

Persona 2

Persona: Vehicle Assembly Fitter

Demographics:
  • 29 years old
  • Married
