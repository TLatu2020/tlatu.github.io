Target Audience

The target audience for this website is broad and includes people who simply want to know what the weather will be today out of curiosity, to people who need to know what the weather will be on a certain day to better plan outdoor events.


Personas

Persona 1

Persona: Mother of two children
Fictional name: Toni Rose
responsibilities: Full-time mother of two children
Demographics:
  • 28 years old
  • Married
  • Mother of two children
  • Full-time mother
Goals and tasks: Majority of her focus is on looking after the children. One of her major concerns when she leaves the house with the kids, is whether they are dressed appropriately for the weather.
Environment: She is busy with trying to juggle two children, while looking after their basic needs such as food and clothing, so she uses her phone on a regular basis because it is more convenient. She does have access to a laptop and does use it occasionally.
Quote: "What is the weather going to be today."

Persona 2

Persona: Vehicle Assembly Fitter
Fictional name: Matt Dee
Job Title : Team Manager
Demographics:
  • 29 years old
  • Married
  • Father of five children
Goals and tasks: As the team manager he is responsible for ensuring proper procedures are followed during certain weather condition such as running safety checks if it is raining or tools down if it gets too hot.
Environment: He is more than comfortable using a computer. He is always connected to he work wifi at work, and has fibre connection at home. He is on the computer for more then 2 hours a day with emails and using the web.
Quote: "Being prepared early, saves us a lot of time."

Scenarios

  1. Scenario one: A mother would like to know roughly what time it may rain, so she will know whether it is worth it to hang her washing or not.
  2. Scenario two: An assembly line manager would like to know roughly what time it will start to hit a certain temperature so they can hopefully adjust to meet quota numbers, before the "tools down" law applies.
  3. Scenario three: Youth Activity Leader would like to know whether the weather on the weekend will be suited for the camping trip they have planned, at a certain camping location.
  4. Scenario four: A mother would like to know what the weather will be like in a certain town to know what clothes will be suitable for her children.
  5. Scenario five: High School students on holidays would like to know what the weather will be like for the following week to know what possible activities they can do for the week.
