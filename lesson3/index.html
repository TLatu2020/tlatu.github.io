Site Purpose


Weather is something that can affect people across all walks of life. A rainy day can be change a mothers day just as much as it can change a teenager, who loves the outdoors of Southeast Idaho, day. Knowing what the weather will be today, tomorrow or even the rest of the week, is very important to avoid undesirable weather that will change your day. So, the purpose of Weathers Downunder is to provide a reliable, user friendly source for people to access information about current and up coming weather patterns, particularly for the Souteast Idaho region.
