Site Purpose

The weather can surprisingly vary often in the land downunder (Australia). One day the weather can be hot with the temperature at 35℃ (95℉), and the very next day it will be heavy rain. These weather changes can be troublesome to daily life, and to events that have been prepared. The purpose of this website is to provide a reliable, user friendly source for the people downunder to access information about current and up coming weather patterns. Not only for the weather downunder, but anywhere they may be.
