The Mountains

With winter now upon us and plenty of snow coming down, we are all excited about the holiday season. The last thing we want to happen is all our plans go to ruin because of last minute changes that are out of our control. So, provided by skisodasprings.com here are the following updates of mountain operations as of 11/28/20:

General operations

  • Soda Springs is open Thursday–Monday and closed Tuesday–Wednesday until the December holiday period.
  • Planet Kids, Tube Town and the Lions Head chairlift open at 9:30AM on weekend days and 10AM on weekdays.
  • Mountain Services and Rental Services begin at 8:30AM.

Soda Springs Base Lodge

  • Access to rental services is available for guests with pre-purchased rental equipment.
  • The retail shop is open with capacity limits in place.
  • Restroom access is available for guests with season passes, daily lift tickets and Mountain Adventure.
  • The Soda Springs Café is open for grab & go food or drink options with online ordering.

West Meadows Lodge and Planet Kids

  • Access to children's rental equipment for Mountain Adventure.
  • Restroom access is available for guests with the Family Pass and Mountain Adventure.

Lift and Mountain Adventure Access

  • Lion’s Head double chairlift is open with beginner terrain only. The lift will be loaded at a single capacity, except for guests that arrived together.
  • Tube Town is open with tubing access for guests ages 8+ (adults too!).
  • The small carpet at Planet Kids is open with tubing access for kids age 7 and under, and skiing and snowboarding for kids in the learning area. Additionally, one tubing carousel and a snowplay volcano are open.
  • The jeep sleigh will be open with shuttle rides for one group at a time. If you arrived together, you'll ride together.

Close services

  • The Snack Shack at Planet Kids is closed.
  • Crystal Bowl triple chairlift is closed
  • The Woodward Start Park is closed.
