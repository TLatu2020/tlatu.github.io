Festivities

There is a lot that to celebrate here in Prestons, so, you will find there are a lot of fetivals and events that occur throughout the year. One such festival is the "Nepolean Dynamite Festival", which is based on the film Nepolean Dynamite that was filmed here. The theme for the festival changes each year, and is based on things that happened in the film. Thetherball Tournament, Impersonation, and a Football Throwing Contest, were some of the festival themes in previous years.

Another event that is held is "The Famous Preston Night Rodeo" which is usually held around the end of July. This event is famous for being the first rodeo to be held at night time, which also helped in the naming of the event. You can expect to watch some of the countries best bull riding while also ejoying yourselves watching barrel racing and other popular events.

You cannot talk about events and festivals of Preston without talking about the "Festival of Lights" held each year, beginning the day after Thanksgiving right up to December 31. A lot is held during the "Festival of Lights", such as a lighted parade, fireworks, and displays held at the city park, to name a few. This is an event you do not want to miss out on.
